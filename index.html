Andy's Tribute

ANDYMKOSI is a multifaceted artist, encompassing the roles of a Documentary Photographer, Filmmaker, and Visual storyteller. Her areas of expertise lie in Behind The Scenes and Black & White Photography, showcasing her dedication to these specific genres. However, her creative vision extends far beyond the camera lens, as she endeavours to unveil the narratives of everyday life and explore thought-provoking social commentary.

Notably, ANDYMKOSI's talent as a photographer has been recognized through her contribution as the cover photographer for the award-winning poetry anthology, "Collective Amnesia." This accomplishment speaks to her ability to capture the essence of the written word through her evocative visual storytelling.
